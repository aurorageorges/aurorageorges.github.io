About me

Hello, my name is Aurora Georges and I'm a norwegian artist and illustrator currently based in Vietnam! Born in 1994, I grew up with a big interest for drawing and after finishing high school with a specialization within art, there was no doubt. I wanted to become better in what I love to do, and so I got my bachelor degree in Digital Art and Design after graduating from Hedmark University of Applied Sciences. I started my career working as a freelancer for Prouder AS creating illustrated logos for half a year before moving to Vietnam.

There I have been working for over a year as a Junior 2D concept artist for Gameloft in Da Nang, and it has been quite the adventure so far! I've been learning everything from how a normal workday look like at a game-company to producing more efficiently concept art and illustrations after feedback.

I'm always looking forward to meet new people and constantly improve my art, so please feel free to contact me about exciting work opportunities!
