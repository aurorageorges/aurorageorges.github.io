About me

Hello, my name is Aurora Georges and I'm a Norwegian artist and illustrator currently based in Vietnam.

Born in 1994, I early discovered a passion for drawing. While specializing in art in high school, I knew I wanted to become better in what I love to do. After obtaining my bachelor degree in Digital Art and Design from Hedmark University of Applied Sciences, I started my career working as a freelancer; creating illustrated logos and portraits.

Currently I'm working as a Junior 2D concept artist for Gameloft in Da Nang and it has been quite an adventure so far! I've been learning to produce more efficiently concept art and illustrations in an overall production process based on communication. Working abroad has also allowed me to experience a new culture as well as an international working environment.

I'm always looking forward to meet new people and constantly improve my art, so please feel free to contact me about work opportunities.
